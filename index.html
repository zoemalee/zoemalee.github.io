A Bit About Me

I am a studying graphic design student at Western Washington University and have been here for the past 3 years just bee bopping around, doing the college thing. I enjoy engaging people with art and design and building connection from human to human. My favorite types of design are Illustration, motion design, and packaging design. Anything conceptual is right up my alley. I am a firm believer in the importance of maintaining your inner child (we adults need to loosen up sometimes). My goal is to make navigating the world we live in an exciting and inspiring experience whether that is scrolling through a website, flipping through a publication, or simply getting excited to try the fun drink that caught your eye on the shelf.
