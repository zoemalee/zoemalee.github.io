BIO

Hi everybody… My name is Zoe, I am a Graphic Design student at Western Washington University in Bellingham. I enjoy illustration, packaging design, and motion design, anything that lets me get a little artsy. Since most of my work-life is stuck behind a computer, any opportunity I get to make analog art, I AM THERE. Drawing, painting, linocut, crochet, collage…you name it, I probably want to learn it. When I’m not glued to my computer or scribbling nonsense into my sketchbook, I love to ski, mountain bike, and spend time outside. I love working with my hands.
